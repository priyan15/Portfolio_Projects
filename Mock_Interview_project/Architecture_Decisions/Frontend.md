## Frontend Decisions:

The purpose of this project is to do applied ML/ AI work. I want the UI to be basic, which just fulfills my functional requirement. Since I want the frontend logic to be quick and simple, I am thinking of building the front end with pure Python. I am thinking Streamlit, alternately thinking using Gradio, Reflex, NiceGUI, Flet.

Initially for MVP, since my major area of interest would be the actual ML work, I will consider Streamlit, may later switch to NiceGUI.
